## ðŸŽ¨ Design Principles for NutriVision AI

### 1. Thai-First, Mobile-First
Design for Thai users on mobile devices as primary experience. Desktop and international markets are secondary.

### 2. Delight Through Simplicity
68% of Thai users prefer easy navigation. Make complex nutrition simple and delightful.

### 3. Visual Over Textual
Use icons, images, charts, and animations. Minimize text. Support video/voice content.

### 4. Trust Through Transparency
Show how AI works, explain scores clearly, cite scientific sources, protect user privacy.

### 5. Motivate Through Progress
Gamify the journey with badges, streaks, challenges, and visual progress tracking.

### 6. Accessible to All
Design for grandmothers and Gen Z alike. Voice navigation, large buttons, high contrast.

### 7. Culturally Authentic
Buddhist values, Thai eating wisdom, LINE integration, PromptPay, Thai AI personality.

### 8. Fast & Reliable
<3 second loads, offline mode, works on 3G, optimized for low-end devices.
