## Critical Success Factors

### Market Differentiation
1. **Thai Food Specialization**: Unique 1000+ Thai recipe database with cultural context

3. **Localized Features**: PromptPay, LINE Login, Thai/English AI chat

- **Subscription Pricing**: Market supports $9.99/month or $49.99/year (MyFitnessPal model)
- **B2B Opportunities**: Corporate wellness market worth THB 2,500 per employee annually

### Strategic Partnerships
- **Food Delivery Integration**: GrabFood (35% market share), LINE MAN (33% market share)

## Key Challenges & Mitigations

### Challenge 1: Regulatory Compliance
- **Risk**: Apps making health claims may be classified as medical devices under Thai FDA
- **Mitigation**: Position as nutrition information tool, avoid therapeutic claims, focus on education

### Challenge 2: Competitive Landscape
- **Risk**: Established players (MyFitnessPal: 200M downloads, 85M MAU)
- **Mitigation**: Leverage Thai market specialization, superior localization, cultural relevance

### Challenge 3: User Retention
- **Risk**: Health apps face high churn rates
- **Mitigation**: Implement gamification, habit formation, personalized experiences, social features
