## 1. Social Media Marketing Strategy

**1. Educational (40% of content)**

  - "The surprising nutrition in Som Tam"
  - "Blood sugar impact of Thai desserts"

**Volume**: 5-7 videos per week (at least 1 per day)
**Duration**: 15-60 seconds (TikTok sweet spot)
**Style**: Raw, authentic, not overly polished
**Trends**: Leverage trending audio, hashtags, challenges

## 2. Influencer Marketing (Deep Dive)

### Influencer Tier Strategy

#### Tier 1: Nano-Influencers (1K-10K followers)
- **Cost**: Free product (THB 0-3,000)
- **Volume**: 50+ creators
- **Benefit**: Authentic, hyper-engaged communities
- **Use Case**: Continuous grassroots campaign

#### Tier 2: Micro-Influencers (10K-50K followers)
- **Cost**: THB 5,000-15,000 per post
- **Volume**: 20-30 creators/month
- **Benefit**: Best engagement rate, niche audiences
- **Use Case**: Core influencer program

#### Tier 3: Mid-Tier Influencers (50K-200K followers)
- **Cost**: THB 20,000-70,000 per post
- **Volume**: 5-10 creators/month
- **Benefit**: Broader reach while maintaining engagement
- **Use Case**: Awareness campaigns

#### Tier 4: Macro-Influencers (200K-1M followers)
- **Cost**: THB 80,000-300,000 per post
- **Volume**: 2-3 per quarter
- **Benefit**: Massive reach, brand credibility
- **Use Case**: Major launches, campaigns

#### Tier 5: Celebrities (1M+ followers)
- **Cost**: THB 500,000-2,000,000+ per post (or equity deal)
- **Volume**: 1 per year (if at all)
- **Benefit**: Mainstream awareness
- **Use Case**: Series B fundraise, major milestone

### Influencer Selection Criteria

**Must-Have**:
- ‚úÖ Authentic content style (not overly promotional)
- ‚úÖ Engaged audience (>3% engagement rate)
- ‚úÖ Thai-speaking audience majority
- ‚úÖ Health/fitness/food niche alignment
- ‚úÖ No conflicts (don't promote junk food, competitors)

**Nice-to-Have**:
- Personal health journey story
- Nutrition knowledge (or willingness to learn)
- Long-term partnership potential
- Content creation skills (video editing, etc.)

### Influencer Collaboration Models

**Model 1: One-Time Post**
- Single sponsored post/video
- Clear disclosure (#ad, #sponsored)
- Deliverable: 1 post + usage rights

**Model 2: Campaign Series**
- 3-5 posts over 1-2 months
- Storytelling arc (trial ‚Üí results)
- Deeper integration

**Model 3: Ambassador Program**
- 6-12 month partnerships
- Monthly content quota
- Exclusive benefits (lifetime premium)
- Equity option for top-tier

**Model 4: Co-Creation**
- Influencer designs recipe pack
- Revenue share model
- Long-term alignment

**Model 5: Affiliate**
- Unique discount codes
- Commission on conversions
- Performance-based (low upfront cost)

### Influencer Campaign Example

**Campaign**: "30-Day Thai Food Transformation"

**Influencer**: Fitness micro-influencer, 40K followers, 5% engagement

**Structure**:
- **Week 1**: Announce challenge, install NutriVision
- **Week 2**: Daily food scans, learning surprises
- **Week 3**: Adjusting diet based on insights
- **Week 4**: Results reveal (weight, energy, blood sugar)
- **Bonus**: Q&A about experience

**Cost**: THB 40,000 (4 posts)
**Expected Results**:
- Reach: 120K+ impressions
- Engagement: 6K+ likes/comments
- Conversions: 200-400 downloads (THB 100-200 CPA)

## 4. User Retention Strategies

### Retention Benchmarks

**Industry Averages** (Health Apps):
- **Day 1**: 50-60% (after download)
- **Day 7**: 30-40%
- **Day 30**: 15-20%
- **Day 90**: 5-10%

**NutriVision Targets**:
- **Day 1**: 65% (strong onboarding)
- **Day 7**: 45% (habit formation)
- **Day 30**: 25% (value demonstrated)
- **Day 90**: 15% (engaged users)

### Retention Strategy Framework

#### A. Onboarding Excellence (Day 1-3)

**Goal**: Get to "Aha Moment" ASAP

**Onboarding Flow**:
1. **Welcome**: Brief value proposition (15 seconds)
2. **Permissions**: Camera, notifications (explain why)
3. **Personalization Quiz**: Goals, preferences, health info (2 minutes)
4. **First Scan Tutorial**: "Try scanning your next meal" (immediate value)
5. **Result Explanation**: Walk through 8-dimension scores
6. **Next Steps**: "Scan 3 more meals today to unlock insights"

**Aha Moment**: First successful Thai food scan with accurate recognition
- User thinks: "Wow, it actually knows what pad krapow is!"
- Metric: % who complete 1+ scan in first session
- Target: >60%

**Onboarding Success Metrics**:
- **Completion Rate**: % who finish onboarding
- **Time to First Scan**: Median time (target: <5 min)
- **First Session Scans**: Average scans in first session (target: 2+)

#### B. Habit Formation (Days 3-30)

**Goal**: Make NutriVision a daily habit

**Habit Loop** (BJ Fogg Model):
- **Trigger**: Meal time, hunger, notification
- **Action**: Open app, scan food (must be EASY)
- **Reward**: Instant insight, progress tracking, achievements

**Tactics**:

**1. Push Notifications** (Personalized)
- **Meal Reminders**: "Lunch time! Scan your meal?" (based on user patterns)
- **Streaks**: "You're on a 5-day streak! Don't break it"
- **Insights**: "Your weekly report is ready"
- **Tips**: "Did you know? [Nutrition fact]"
- **Timing**: Personalized to user's meal times
- **Frequency**: Max 2 per day (avoid spam)
- **Opt-out**: Easy to customize notification preferences

**2. Streaks & Gamification**
- **Daily Scan Streak**: "Scanned 7 days in a row! üî•"
- **Weekly Goal**: "3 out of 7 days this week"
- **Badges**: "Bronze Scanner" (10 scans), "Silver Scanner" (50 scans)
- **Leaderboard**: "You're in top 20% of users this week"
- **Visual**: Flame icon (like Duolingo), progress bar

**3. Progress Visualization**
- **Weekly Summary**: "This week vs last week"
- **Trends**: Charts showing nutrition improving over time
- **Milestones**: "You've scanned 50 meals! Here's what we learned..."
- **Goal Tracking**: Visual progress toward weight/health goals

**4. Social Features**
- **Share Meals**: Share scan results to Instagram Story (branded template)
- **Challenges**: "30-Day Blood Sugar Challenge" with friends
- **Community**: In-app feed of user meals (optional, privacy-controlled)
- **Comments & Likes**: Supportive community engagement

**5. Personalized Content**
- **In-App Tips**: Based on eating patterns
  - "We noticed you eat late dinners. Here's why that affects sleep..."
- **Recipe Recommendations**: "Based on your scans, try these recipes"
- **Education**: Bite-sized lessons on nutrition
- **AI Coach Check-Ins**: "How are you feeling this week?"

#### C. Value Reinforcement (Days 30-90)

**Goal**: Demonstrate ROI, build long-term commitment

**Tactics**:

**1. Progress Reports**
- **Monthly Report**: "Your January Nutrition Report"
  - Total scans
  - Top foods eaten
  - Nutrition trends (protein intake up 20%!)
  - Goal progress
  - Recommendations for next month
- **Quarterly Report**: Deeper insights, health trends
- **Shareable**: "Share your progress" (social proof)

**2. Outcome Tracking**
- **Weight Changes**: If goal is weight loss, show trend
- **Energy Levels**: "How do you feel?" daily check-in
- **Health Metrics**: If integrated with wearables, show correlations
  - "When you eat high-protein breakfasts, your afternoon energy is 30% better"
- **Personalized Insights**: AI finds patterns user didn't notice

**3. Premium Feature Trials**
- **Free Trial Extension**: "Enjoy 1 more week of premium"
- **Feature Highlight**: "This premium feature helped users like you..."
- **Limited-Time Offer**: "Upgrade now, get 30% off annual"

**4. Winback Campaigns** (for churned users)
- **30 days inactive**: "We miss you! Here's what's new..."
- **60 days inactive**: "Come back and get 50% off premium for 3 months"
- **90 days inactive**: Final attempt, special offer

## 5. Gamification Strategy

### Game Mechanics

**1. Points System**
- **Earn Points for**:
  - Daily scan (10 points)
  - Scanning healthy meal (bonus 5 points)
  - 7-day streak (50 bonus points)
  - Completing weekly goal (100 points)
  - Inviting friend (200 points)
- **Spend Points on**:
  - Unlock premium recipes (500 points)
  - Custom meal plans (1,000 points)
  - 1 month premium (2,000 points)

**2. Badges & Achievements**
- **Scan Milestones**: 10, 50, 100, 500, 1,000 scans
- **Streak Achievements**: 7, 30, 100, 365 days
- **Health Achievements**: "Blood Sugar Master" (maintain good scores)
- **Social Achievements**: "Influencer" (10 friends joined)
- **Collection**: "Tried 100 different Thai dishes"

**3. Levels & Progression**
- **Levels**: Novice ‚Üí Apprentice ‚Üí Expert ‚Üí Master ‚Üí Guru
- **Level Up**: Based on scans, streaks, engagement
- **Perks**: Each level unlocks features, badges, discounts

**4. Challenges**
- **Personal Challenges**: "Scan 5 days this week"
- **Community Challenges**: "100,000 scans this month as a community"
- **Competitive Challenges**: "Top 100 scorers this week"
- **Themed Challenges**: "30-Day Plant-Based Challenge"
- **Rewards**: Badges, points, premium access, prizes

**5. Leaderboards**
- **Daily / Weekly / Monthly**
- **Categories**: Most scans, highest average scores, longest streaks
- **Privacy**: Opt-in, use usernames not real names
- **Incentive**: Top 10 get special badge/premium access

### Gamification Best Practices

**Do's**:
- ‚úÖ Make earning rewards EASY initially (quick wins)
- ‚úÖ Show progress visually (bars, charts, icons)
- ‚úÖ Celebrate achievements (confetti, sounds)
- ‚úÖ Variety (multiple ways to earn rewards)
- ‚úÖ Clear goals (know what to do next)

**Don'ts**:
- ‚ùå Make it feel like work (should be fun)
- ‚ùå Too complex (simple > complicated)
- ‚ùå Pay-to-win (fairness important)
- ‚ùå Punish (no negative points or shaming)
- ‚ùå Spam with notifications (respect attention)

  - "Hi Ploy, we noticed you love Som Tam..."
- **Mobile-Optimized**: 60%+ open emails on mobile
- **Clear CTA**: One primary action per email
- **Value-First**: Educational > promotional (80/20 rule)

## 9. Paid Advertising Strategy

### Budget Allocation (Year 1: THB 3M marketing)

**Channel Split**:
- **TikTok Ads**: 40% (THB 1.2M)
- **Instagram Ads**: 30% (THB 900K)
- **Facebook Ads**: 15% (THB 450K)
- **Google Ads**: 10% (THB 300K)
- **LINE Ads**: 5% (THB 150K)

### Campaign Structure

**Awareness Campaigns** (Top of Funnel):
- **Objective**: Brand awareness, reach
- **Content**: Video ads, engaging content
- **Targeting**: Broad interest (health, food)
- **KPI**: CPM (cost per 1000 impressions) <THB 100

**Consideration Campaigns** (Middle of Funnel):
- **Objective**: Engagement, traffic
- **Content**: Feature highlights, testimonials
- **Targeting**: Engaged audiences, website visitors
- **KPI**: CPC (cost per click) <THB 8

**Conversion Campaigns** (Bottom of Funnel):
- **Objective**: App installs, signups
- **Content**: Direct CTA, free trial offers
- **Targeting**: High-intent (retargeting, lookalikes)
- **KPI**: CPA (cost per acquisition) <THB 400

### Creative Testing Framework

**Test Variables**:
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds (5 variations)
- **Visual Style**: Animated vs live action
- **Length**: 15s vs 30s vs 60s
- **CTA**: "Download Now" vs "Try Free" vs "Learn More"
- **Audience**: Different demographic segments

**Testing Process**:
1. **Launch**: 5 ad variations with equal budget
2. **Learn** (3 days): Identify winner (CTR, CPA)
3. **Optimize**: Kill losers, double down on winner
4. **Scale**: Increase budget on winner
5. **Iterate**: Create variations of winner, repeat

### Retargeting Strategy

**Retargeting Audiences**:
- **Website Visitors**: Didn't download app
- **App Installers**: Didn't complete onboarding
- **Free Users**: Haven't upgraded (after 14 days)
- **Churned Users**: Haven't opened app in 30 days

**Retargeting Content**:
- **Urgency**: "Limited time offer"
- **Social Proof**: "Join 10,000 users"
- **Value Reinforcement**: "See what you're missing"
- **Discount**: Special offer for returning users
