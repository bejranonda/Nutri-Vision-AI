- Most apps use Western food databases

## Market Challenges

### 1. Regulatory Uncertainty
- Medical device classification risks for health claims
- Data privacy compliance (PDPA requirements)
- Food safety regulations
- Cross-border data restrictions

### 2. User Retention
- High churn rates in health apps
- Requires continuous engagement
- Behavior change is difficult
- Competition for user attention

### 3. Monetization Complexity
- Freemium model challenges
- Pricing sensitivity in emerging markets
- Balancing free vs. premium features
- Customer acquisition costs

### 4. Technical Challenges
- Food recognition accuracy for local cuisines
- Portion size estimation difficulties
- Database maintenance (Thai foods)
- API costs (Google Gemini, AI services)
