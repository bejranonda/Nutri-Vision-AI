### 8. [Regulatory & Compliance](./08-regulatory-compliance.md)
**Legal Requirements | 35 min read**

Regulatory landscape including PDPA, medical device regulations, and compliance requirements.

**Key Regulations:**

**Thailand PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act)**
- Effective: June 1, 2022
- Health data = sensitive personal data
- Explicit consent required
- Data breach notification: 72 hours
- Penalties: THB 500K - 5M + possible imprisonment

**Medical Device Classification**
- Risk: Apps making medical claims = medical device
- Strategy: Position as "nutrition information tool"
- Avoid: "Treats", "cures", "diagnoses" language
- Use: "Educational", "informational" framing

**Compliance Costs:**
- Year 1: THB 800K - 1.2M
- Year 2: THB 1.5M - 2M
- Year 3: THB 2.5M - 3.5M

**Required:**
- Privacy Policy (Thai + English)
- Terms of Service
- Consent management system
- Data encryption
- Security audits
