## 1. PRODUCT DIRECTION & STRATEGIC VISION

### 1.1 North Star Metric

**Primary Success Metric:** **Weekly Active Users (WAU) who Complete 3+ Food Scans**

**Rationale:**
- Indicates product value realization (not just install)
- Predicts conversion to paid (habit formation)
- Measurable behavior change (nutrition awareness)
- Aligns with mission (helping Thais eat healthier)

**Supporting Metrics:**
- User Retention Rate (Day 30, Day 90)
- Free-to-Paid Conversion Rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)
- Blood Sugar Awareness Index (proprietary metric)

### 1.2 Strategic Vision

**3-Year Vision Statement:**
> "By 2028, NutriVision AI is the trusted nutrition companion for 1 million Thai families, preventing 100,000 cases of diabetes through AI-powered food intelligence and cultural relevance."

**Mission Evolution:**

| Phase | Timeframe | Mission Focus |
|-------|-----------|---------------|
| **Phase 1: Prove** | Months 1-6 | Validate that Thais want AI nutrition guidance for Thai food |
| **Phase 2: Scale** | Months 6-18 | Become Thailand's #1 nutrition app through superior Thai food knowledge |
| **Phase 3: Lead** | Months 18-36 | Lead Southeast Asia's nutrition technology revolution |
| **Phase 4: Transform** | Year 3+ | Transform preventive health outcomes through AI + community + healthcare integration |

### 1.3 Jobs-to-be-Done Framework

**Primary Job:** *"Help me make healthier food choices that fit my Thai lifestyle without sacrificing the foods I love."*

**Functional Jobs:**
1. **Understand what I'm eating** - Know the nutritional impact of Thai dishes instantly
2. **Manage my blood sugar** - Prevent diabetes/manage existing condition through food choices
3. **Track my nutrition** - Monitor calories, macros, and health trends effortlessly
4. **Plan healthier meals** - Get Thai recipes that align with my health goals
5. **Get expert guidance** - Access nutrition knowledge without expensive consultations

**Emotional Jobs:**
1. **Feel in control** - Confidence in food decisions, agency over health outcomes
2. **Avoid guilt** - Enjoy Thai food without shame or anxiety
3. **Feel proud** - Accomplish health goals, see visible progress
4. **Belong** - Connect with community of health-conscious Thais
5. **Feel cared for** - Personalized attention from AI coach "that understands me"

**Social Jobs:**
1. **Share success** - Inspire family/friends with transformation
2. **Protect loved ones** - Help family (especially kids) eat healthier
3. **Set example** - Be the health-conscious person in social circles
4. **Participate in culture** - Maintain Thai food traditions while being healthy

**Key Insight:** Users aren't hiring us just to "count calories" (functional). They're hiring us to "feel confident eating Thai food while pursuing health goals" (emotional + social + functional).

## 2. VALUE PROPOSITION FRAMEWORK

### 2.1 Value Proposition Canvas

**For:** Health-conscious Thai Millennials & Gen Z (primary segment)

**Who:** Want to eat healthier and prevent lifestyle diseases but struggle with:
- Confusion about Thai food nutrition (no reliable sources)
- Time-consuming manual food tracking (barrier to consistency)
- Western diet advice that doesn't fit Thai eating culture
- Lack of blood sugar awareness (critical for Thai rice-heavy diet)
- Expensive nutrition consultations (THB 1,500-3,000/session)

**NutriVision AI is:** An AI-powered nutrition coach specialized in Thai food

**That provides:** Instant food recognition, 8-dimension health scoring, personalized guidance, and meal planning - all in Thai language

**Unlike:** MyFitnessPal, Noom, and other generic apps that don't understand Thai food or culture

**Our product:** Recognizes 1,000+ Thai dishes with 90%+ accuracy, provides blood sugar impact scores specific to Thai diet patterns, and costs 45-92% less than international competitors

**Key Differentiators:**
1. **Thai Food Specialization** - Only app with deep Thai cuisine knowledge (1,000+ recipes, regional variations, traditional wisdom)
2. **Blood Sugar Focus** - Critical for Thai population (11.6% diabetes prevalence, rice-heavy diet)
3. **Eating Sequence Education** - Proven 70% blood sugar reduction method
4. **Cultural Relevance** - LINE integration, Thai payment methods, culturally appropriate advice
5. **AI Simplicity** - Just snap a photo vs tedious manual entry
6. **Affordable Premium** - THB 199/month vs competitors at THB 350-2,000/month

### 4.4 Kano Model Analysis

**Kano Model categorizes features by customer satisfaction impact:**

**MUST-HAVE FEATURES** (Basic/Threshold)
*Customers expect these. Presence doesn't increase satisfaction, but absence causes major dissatisfaction.*

| Feature | Rationale | Customer Reaction if Missing |
|---------|-----------|----------------------------|
| Food recognition accuracy (90%+) | Core value proposition | "This app doesn't even work" |
| Thai language support | 50%+ of users only speak Thai | "Not for me" (abandonment) |
| Free tier | Industry standard, try-before-buy | "Why should I pay without trying?" |
| Fast scan speed (<5 sec) | Impatience threshold | "Too slow, I'll just guess" |
| Privacy/security (PDPA compliant) | Legal requirement + trust | "I don't trust this app with my data" |
| Basic nutrition info (calories, macros) | Expected functionality | "What's the point of this app?" |

---

**PERFORMANCE FEATURES** (One-Dimensional)
*Satisfaction scales linearly with feature sophistication. More = Better.*

| Feature | Low Performance (Dissatisfaction) | High Performance (Satisfaction) |
|---------|----------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| Food database size | "Too many dishes not recognized" | "It knows every Thai dish I try!" |
| Number of recipes | "Only 50 recipes? That's nothing." | "1,000+ recipes - I'll never get bored!" |
| AI chat responsiveness | "The AI doesn't understand me" | "The AI actually gives great advice!" |
| Personalization accuracy | "Generic advice, not for me" | "It's like it knows exactly what I need!" |
| Dashboard insights | "Just basic charts" | "Wow, I can see trends I never noticed!" |

---

**DELIGHTERS** (Attractive/Excitement)
*Customers don't expect these. Presence creates delight and loyalty. Absence doesn't cause dissatisfaction.*

| Feature | Surprise Factor | Emotional Response |
|---------|----------------|-------------------|
| Eating sequence education (70% blood sugar reduction) | "I never knew this!" | "This is life-changing for my diabetes risk!" |
| Thai food cultural insights (festival foods, traditions) | "No app has this!" | "Finally someone understands Thai culture!" |
| Gamification (streaks, badges, levels) | "Nutrition tracking is fun?" | "I'm addicted to my 30-day streak!" |
| Voice food logging in Thai | "I can just talk to it?" | "This is so convenient!" |
| Family health dashboard | "Track my whole family?" | "Perfect for managing everyone's health!" |
| CGM integration (future) | "Real-time blood sugar?" | "This is the future of nutrition!" |
| AR scanning (future) | "Point and see nutrition?" | "This feels like sci-fi!" |

**Strategic Insight:** Focus development on:
1. **Must-Haves first** (prevent dissatisfaction)
2. **Delighters second** (create wow moments for virality and differentiation)
3. **Performance features third** (incremental improvements)

**Rationale:** Must-Haves prevent churn. Delighters drive word-of-mouth growth and brand love. Performance features are continuous improvement.
