## 1. EXECUTIVE OVERVIEW

### The Opportunity

NutriVision AI enters a **$4.89B global market (2025) growing to $21.54B by 2034** (17.9% CAGR). In Thailand specifically, **66.8% of the population uses health/wellness apps**, creating a **THB 50B ($1.4B) digital health market** with **33M active health app users**.

**No dominant player specializes in Thai cuisine**, creating a clear market gap for a Thai-focused AI nutrition platform.

### The Product

An AI-powered nutrition coach specialized in Thai cuisine that:
- **Recognizes 1,000+ Thai dishes** with 90%+ accuracy using Google Gemini Vision
- **Provides 8-dimension health scoring** (blood sugar, gut health, inflammation, etc.)
- **Delivers personalized guidance** in Thai language through AI chat
- **Costs 45-92% less** than international competitors (THB 199/month vs $9.99-$59)

### The Advantage

**Unique Positioning:** Only app combining:
1. Deep Thai food knowledge (1,000+ recipes, regional variations, cultural context)
2. Blood sugar management focus (critical for 11.6% Thai diabetes prevalence)
3. AI-powered simplicity (3-second scan vs 5-minute manual entry)
4. Cultural integration (LINE, PromptPay, Thai language AI)
5. Affordable premium pricing (THB 199 vs competitors' THB 350-2,000/month)

### The Ask

**Seed Funding:** THB 15-20M ($420-560K)
**Timeline:** Months 3-6
**Use:** Marketing (40%), Technology (20%), Personnel (30%), Operations (10%)
**Runway:** 15-20 months to Series A readiness
