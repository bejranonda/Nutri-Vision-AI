## Revenue Model Options

### 1. Freemium Subscription Model (PRIMARY)

#### Industry Benchmarks
- **Conversion Rate**: 3-5% industry average, 6-8% exceptional performers
- **Trial Conversions**: 8-12% good, 15-25% great
- **Free-to-Paid Timeline**: Most decisions happen within 72 hours

#### Pricing Psychology Insights
- **Loss Aversion**: Trial countdown timers create urgency
- **Social Proof**: Specific testimonials (e.g., "lost 5kg in 2 months") outperform generic praise
- **Anchoring Effect**: Middle-tier pricing appears most attractive
- **Annual Discount**: "Save 20% with annual" reduces hesitation

**Value**: Demonstrates core value, creates habit, drives conversion

**Pricing**:

### Pricing Psychology

#### Why THB 199/month Works:
1. **Below $6 threshold**: Feels like impulse purchase
2. **Below competitors**: 45% cheaper than MyFitnessPal monthly
3. **Anchoring**: Against Noom's $59, looks extremely affordable
4. **Thai purchasing power**: Equivalent to 1-2 cafe drinks
5. **Psychological pricing**: THB 199 vs 200 (just under THB 200 barrier)

#### Annual Discount Strategy:
- **29% discount** (THB 1,699 vs THB 2,388 monthly)
- Creates strong incentive for commitment
- Improves cash flow and LTV
- Reduces churn (harder to cancel annual)
- Competitive with industry (30-40% annual discounts common)

## Conversion Optimization Strategy

### Free-to-Paid Triggers

#### Immediate Value Demonstration (Days 1-3)
- Successful first food scan with impressive accuracy
- "Aha moment": Thai food recognized perfectly
- Immediate actionable insight about meal

#### Feature Limitation at Key Moment (Days 3-7)
- User wants to see full 8-dimension breakdown → Paywall
- User wants to plan meals → Paywall
- User wants to track progress → Paywall
- **Timing**: At moment of high intent

#### Social Proof & Urgency (Days 7-14)
- Show others' success stories
- Limited-time discount (20% off annual if subscribe within 14 days)
- "2,547 people upgraded this month"

#### Personalized Outreach (Days 14-30)
- AI coach message: "I noticed you're interested in blood sugar. Premium users get personalized blood sugar management plans."
- Email: Case study relevant to user's goals

### Key Conversion Metrics to Track

1. **Activation Rate**: % who complete first scan (Target: >60%)
2. **Aha Moment Rate**: % who scan 3+ times in first week (Target: >40%)
3. **Trial Start Rate**: % who hit paywall and see pricing (Target: >30%)
4. **Free-to-Paid**: % who convert (Target: >4%)
5. **Time to Conversion**: Average days to upgrade (Target: <21 days)

## Pricing Experiments to Run

### Year 1 A/B Tests

**Test 1: Annual Discount Level**
- Control: 29% discount (THB 1,699)
- Variant A: 35% discount (THB 1,549)
- Variant B: 40% discount (THB 1,433)
- **Measure**: Annual vs monthly split, overall revenue

**Test 2: Free Tier Scan Limit**
- Control: 10 scans/month
- Variant A: 5 scans/month (more restrictive)
- Variant B: 20 scans/month (more generous)
- **Measure**: Conversion rate, user satisfaction

**Test 3: Pricing Page Layout**
- Control: 3 tiers (Free, Premium, Family)
- Variant A: 2 tiers (Free, Premium - Family as add-on)
- Variant B: 4 tiers (Free, Basic, Premium, Family)
- **Measure**: Click-through and conversion

**Test 4: Promotional Offers**
- Control: No promo
- Variant A: "First month THB 99" (50% off)
- Variant B: "7-day premium trial" then full price
- **Measure**: Trial-to-paid rate, LTV impact
