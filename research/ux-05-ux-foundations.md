### Primary Persona 1: "Health-Conscious Ploy" (Millennial Professional)

**Demographics**:
- Age: 28
- Gender: Female

**App Usage Patterns**:
- Opens health apps during commute (BTS)

- Checks progress before bed
- Shares achievements on Instagram Stories

**Motivations**:
- See visible health improvements
- Learn which Thai foods are healthy
- Quick and easy (no time for complexity)
- Social validation (share progress)

**Frustrations with Current Apps**:
- MyFitnessPal: "Takes forever to log Pad Krapow"

**Jobs to Be Done**:
- âœ… "When I eat Thai food, I want to know its health impact immediately"

- âœ… "When I see progress, I want to share it with friends"

**UX Priorities**:

### Journey Map 2: Lapsed User (Recovering from Churn)

**Scenario**: User signed up, used for 3 days, then stopped

#### Week 1: Active Usage

- Day 1: Scanned 2 meals (excited)
- Day 2: Scanned 1 meal (still engaged)
- Day 3: Scanned 1 meal (habit forming)

- Day 4: Forgot to scan (busy at work)

- No push notification reminder (didn't enable)
- Didn't form strong habit yet
- Competing priorities (work stress)
